Prevalence of rhesus negativity in central Hospital Warri

This study investigated the prevalence of Rhesus-D (Rh-D) negativity among pregnant women attending antenatal care at Central Hospital Warri. Using a descriptive cross-sectional design, blood samples from 400 women were analyzed to determine their ABO and Rh-D blood group status. Results showed a Rh-D negativity rate of 14.5%, significantly higher than Nigeria’s national average of 2.5–9.5%.

Pedaling Profits: Uncovering What Drives Bike Sales

This interactive Power BI dashboard analyzes bike purchase patterns across demographics, regions, income levels, occupations, and commute distances. The goal was to uncover key factors influencing buying behavior and provide actionable insights for strategic marketing. Key insights include: Middle-aged professionals with higher income are the primary buyers. Most purchases occur in North America and Europe. Bike sales are highest among those commuting 2 to 10 miles daily. The dashboard uses slicers for gender, home ownership, and car ownership to support dynamic filtering and segmentation. This project demonstrates my ability to transform raw data into meaningful visual stories that support business decision-making.
